3. Problem & Opportunity

The Vietnamese diaspora sends an estimated ~USD 18B/year in remittances home, yet there is no trusted, transparent, diaspora-native platform that channels both money and mentorship into verified talent and impact. Existing options are fragmented: generic crowdfunding (no verification, no journey), informal family transfers (no transparency), and traditional charities (limited diaspora-specific design). DSTS combines verification, structured journeys, and transparent reporting in one bilingual ecosystem.
