Launch Campaign Preparation

Kế hoạch marketing launch DSTS: đảm bảo campaign có chiến lược rõ ràng, timeline cụ thể và KPI đo lường được.

Version: v1.0 Cập nhật: 2026-06-16 Status: In Preparation

1. Campaign Objectives

  • Brand Awareness: Tăng nhận biết DSTS trong cộng đồng người Việt toàn cầu
  • User Acquisition: Thu hút 1,000+ users đăng ký trong tháng đầu
  • Content Engagement: Đạt 10,000+ page views trong tháng đầu
  • Community Building: Tích lũy 500+ email subscribers
  • Trust Building: Xây dựng uy tín thông qua content chất lượng

2. Target Audience

  • Primary: Người Việt toàn cầu (25-45 tuổi), quan tâm đến phát triển bản thân, sáng tạo, cộng đồng
  • Secondary: Creators, mentors, sponsors muốn kết nối với cộng đồng người Việt
  • Tertiary: Organizations, NGOs muốn hợp tác với DSTS

3. Campaign Timeline

Phase 1: Pre-Launch (2 tuần trước launch)

Teaser content, waitlist signup, social media预热, press outreach

Phase 2: Launch Week (tuần launch)

Official announcement, press release, social media blitz, email campaign, influencer outreach

Phase 3: Post-Launch (4 tuần sau launch)

Content series, community building, user onboarding, feedback collection, optimization

4. Content Strategy

  • Blog Posts: 2-3 bài/tuần về DSTS vision, stories, insights
  • Social Media: Daily posts trên Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X
  • Email Newsletter: Weekly newsletter với curated content
  • Video Content: YouTube shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok về DSTS
  • Press Coverage: Pitch to Vietnamese media outlets

5. Press Kit

  • Press Release: Official announcement press release
  • Media Kit: Logo, brand guidelines, images, bios
  • FAQs: Frequently asked questions for media
  • Story Ideas: Suggested story angles for journalists
  • Contact Info: Media contact information

6. Social Media Strategy

  • Facebook: DSTS page, groups, ads targeting Vietnamese diaspora
  • Instagram: Visual content, stories, reels, influencer partnerships
  • LinkedIn: Professional content, thought leadership, B2B outreach
  • Twitter/X: Real-time updates, community engagement, hashtag campaigns
  • TikTok: Short-form video content, viral potential

7. Email Marketing

  • Waitlist Email: Welcome email với DSTS vision
  • Launch Announcement: Official launch announcement
  • Content Series: Weekly curated content emails
  • User Onboarding: Onboarding email sequence
  • Re-engagement: Re-engagement campaigns for inactive users

8. Analytics & KPIs

  • Website Analytics: Google Analytics 4, page views, sessions, bounce rate
  • Social Media Analytics: Followers, engagement, reach, impressions
  • Email Analytics: Open rate, click rate, unsubscribe rate
  • Conversion Metrics: Sign-ups, content engagement, time on site
  • Brand Metrics: Brand mentions, sentiment analysis, share of voice

9. Budget Allocation

  • Content Creation: 30% - Writers, designers, video editors
  • Social Media Ads: 25% - Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn ads
  • Influencer Marketing: 20% - Influencer partnerships
  • Email Marketing: 10% - Email platform, automation
  • PR & Media: 10% - Press outreach, media relations
  • Analytics & Tools: 5% - Analytics tools, monitoring

10. Risk Management

  • Brand Risk: Negative sentiment, PR crises
  • Platform Risk: Social media algorithm changes, platform downtime
  • Budget Risk: Overspending, low ROI
  • Timing Risk: Launch timing conflicts with major events
  • Content Risk: Low-quality content, negative feedback
