Incident Response Plan

Quy trình xử lý sự cố của DSTS: đảm bảo phản hồi nhanh, minh bạch và hiệu quả khi có sự cố xảy ra.

Version: v1.0 Cập nhật: 2026-06-16 Status: Active

1. Phân loại Incident Severity

SEV1 - Critical: Service hoàn toàn down, data loss, security breach, child safety incident
  • Response time: 15 phút
  • Escalation: C-level, Founder
  • Communication: Public announcement trong 1 giờ
SEV2 - High: Service degradation, partial outage, performance issues
  • Response time: 30 phút
  • Escalation: VP Engineering, Trust Officer
  • Communication: Status page update trong 2 giờ
SEV3 - Medium: Minor issues, non-critical bugs, UI issues
  • Response time: 2 giờ
  • Escalation: Team Lead
  • Communication: Internal tracking, no public announcement

2. Incident Response Process

Step 1: Detection & Triage (0-15 phút)

Monitor alerts → Assess severity → Declare incident → Notify on-call team

Step 2: Investigation (15-60 phút)

Gather logs → Identify root cause → Assess impact → Determine mitigation strategy

Step 3: Mitigation (60 phút - 4 giờ)

Implement temporary fix → Restore service → Monitor stability → Document actions

Step 4: Resolution (4-24 giờ)

Implement permanent fix → Test thoroughly → Deploy to production → Verify resolution

Step 5: Post-Incident (24-48 giờ)

Conduct post-mortem → Document lessons learned → Update processes → Communicate findings

3. Escalation Paths

  • Level 1: On-call engineer → Team Lead
  • Level 2: Team Lead → VP Engineering
  • Level 3: VP Engineering → CTO/Founder
  • Level 4: CTO/Founder → Board/Advisors

4. Communication Plan

  • Internal: Slack #incidents channel, email to all-staff
  • External: Status page, Twitter/X, email to affected users
  • Stakeholders: Direct communication to key stakeholders
  • Media: Prepared statements for media inquiries

5. Child Safety Incidents

Child safety incidents được xử lý theo quy trình đặc biệt:

  • Immediate: Report to cơ quan chức năng (nếu cần thiết)
  • Response: 15 phút response time, 24/7 availability
  • Escalation: Direct to Founder, Trust Officer, Legal Counsel
  • Communication: Limited communication to protect privacy

6. Security Incidents

Security incidents được xử lý theo quy trình đặc biệt:

  • Immediate: Isolate affected systems, preserve evidence
  • Response: 15 phút response time, security team activation
  • Escalation: Direct to CTO, Founder, Legal Counsel
  • Communication: Transparent communication after assessment
