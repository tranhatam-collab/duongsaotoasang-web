## Mandatory legal locks
- points are not money
- points are not investment units
- points are not cash-convertible in phase 1
- user pays for access, not ownership of content
- no leak / record / reupload of paid content
- referral fraud leads to lock or clawback
- creators are responsible for rights to uploaded content
- DSTS has moderation rights
- revenue share is calculated on net revenue
- events may change schedule / format / guest when needed
- no promise of private access unless explicitly included in the package

## Refund principle
- subscriptions may have a limited refund window if Founder chooses
- point purchases are generally non-refundable once used
- tickets and experiences follow event-specific policy
- refunds trigger point and referral clawback logic

## App/store posture
- app phase 1 is engagement-first
- no assumption of direct in-app purchase until store policy path is approved
- web-first payment remains default until mobile purchase strategy is separately locked
