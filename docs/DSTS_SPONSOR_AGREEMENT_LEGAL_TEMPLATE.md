## 5. RED FLAG ESCALATION (REFUSE LIST)

DSTS shall refuse sponsorship from organizations primarily engaged in:

- ❌ Tobacco, vaping, e-cigarette manufacturing
- ❌ Gambling, casino, sports betting (except regulated charity raffle)
- ❌ Predatory finance (payday loan > 36% APR, predatory lending)
- ❌ Adult entertainment, pornography
- ❌ Anti-LGBT advocacy organizations
- ❌ Weapons manufacturing (except dual-use legitimate Sponsor with separate ethics review)
- ❌ Cryptocurrency exchange without proper regulatory registration
- ❌ Pyramid scheme / Multi-level marketing (MLM)
- ❌ Organizations under active SEC enforcement, OFAC sanction, or material legal investigation
- ❌ Organizations explicitly hostile to Vietnamese diaspora or DSTS mission
