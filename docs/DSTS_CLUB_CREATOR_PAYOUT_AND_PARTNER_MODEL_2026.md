# DSTS CLUB CREATOR PAYOUT AND PARTNER MODEL 2026

## Creator categories
- Artists / entertainers
- Entrepreneurs
- Experts
- Thought leaders
- Community contributors
- Vietnamese global achievers
- Special guest creators

## Participation models
### Model A — Membership pool creator
Creator contributes to the club library and shares in creator pool.

### Model B — Event creator
Creator hosts specific talks or sessions and receives event-based revenue share.

### Model C — Premium experience creator
Creator offers small-room, dinner, mastermind or other premium access experiences.

### Model D — Sponsored / campaign creator
Creator participates in sponsor-backed campaigns under separate rules.

## Payout principles
- payout is separate from points
- payout follows signed creator agreement
- payout is based on net revenue
- payout timing is fixed by policy
- payout includes refund / chargeback reserve window
- payout transparency dashboard is required

## Creator dashboard must show
- gross attributed revenue
- fee deductions
- refunds / chargebacks
- net revenue
- pool formula factors
- event revenue
- premium experience revenue
- payout status
- creator points
- content performance
- member growth

## Sponsor / partner model
Sponsor packages may support:
- talk show series
- creator campaigns
- diaspora spotlight series
- themed content seasons
- special event support
- reward sponsorship

Sponsor must not:
- control payout rules outside signed agreement
- convert rewards into securities-like language
- override moderation or trust rules
- create hidden paid endorsement without disclosure
