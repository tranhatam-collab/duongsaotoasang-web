## Reward posture
Points are loyalty / access / reward points.
Points are not cash, investment units, tradable tokens or guaranteed cash equivalents.

## Referral logic

- Reward only on verified conversion
- Add retention bonuses
- Apply clawback on refund/fraud
- Support pending → earned → clawed_back states
