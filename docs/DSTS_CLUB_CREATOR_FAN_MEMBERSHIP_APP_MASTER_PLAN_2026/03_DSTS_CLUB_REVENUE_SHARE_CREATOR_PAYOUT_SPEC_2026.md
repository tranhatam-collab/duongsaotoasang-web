# DSTS CLUB — REVENUE SHARE, CREATOR PAYOUT & CREATOR SCORING SPEC 2026

## 1. Nguyên tắc chung

Không dòng doanh thu nào được chia tiền nếu chưa xác định gross revenue, payment fee, refund, chargeback, tax, direct event cost nếu có, net revenue, revenue share rule, payout schedule, withholding/tax responsibility, invoice/receipt.

Tất cả split tính trên **NET REVENUE**.

## 2. 7 nguồn doanh thu

1. Membership subscription.
2. Talk show ticket.
3. Points purchase.
4. Premium experiences.
5. Sponsorship / partner packages.
6. Multi-creator bundles.
7. Digital products / archive packs.

## 3. Membership pool

```text
50% DSTS platform / operation
30% Creator Pool
20% Growth / community / reserve
```

Creator Pool formula:

```text
Creator Share Score =
  30% valid watch time
+ 20% retention impact
+ 15% premium conversion
+ 15% event participation
+ 10% community quality
+ 10% creator contribution score
- penalties
```

Penalties: refund spike, complaint rate cao, content violation, low completion quality, fraud engagement, missed commitment.

## 4. Talk show / premium room split

Mặc định:

```text
60% creator / speaker
40% platform
```

Nếu DSTS gánh production lớn:

```text
50/50 hoặc 55 creator / 45 platform
```

## 5. Private premium experience split

Mặc định:

```text
70% creator
30% platform
```

Áp dụng cho meet & greet, private dinner, small room, business salon, mastermind, legacy conversation.

## 6. Points purchase revenue

Points purchase là doanh thu thương mại của platform trừ khi có campaign riêng. Không tự động chia cho creator.

Creator chỉ được hưởng nếu point package gắn với creator-specific campaign, reward redemption của creator, hợp đồng ghi rõ.

## 7. Sponsor revenue

Platform sponsor: tài trợ cho DSTS Club chung, thuộc platform revenue.

Creator campaign sponsor: tài trợ cho creator/series cụ thể, split theo hợp đồng riêng.

## 8. Creator payout lifecycle

Trạng thái payout:

```text
accrued
pending_review
approved
paid
held
disputed
reversed
```

Chu kỳ đề xuất: monthly payout. Threshold: 1.000.000 VND hoặc 50 USD. Hold period: 14–30 ngày.

Không payout nếu creator chưa ký agreement, vi phạm bản quyền, fraud/refund bất thường, complaint nghiêm trọng, payment identity không khớp, legal hold.

## 9. Creator Points và payout

Creator Points không phải tiền. Creator payout không được tính trực tiếp từ Creator Points. Creator Points là contribution signals, không phải cash equivalents.

## 10. Creator dashboard cần có

Gross revenue, net revenue, estimated payout, refund/chargeback hold, content performance, membership impact, talk show sales, reward redemptions, Creator Points, payout history, policy notes.

## 11. Hợp đồng creator phải ghi rõ

Creator ownership, license to DSTS, public preview rights, paid content rights, clip/trailer rights, revenue share model, net revenue definition, payout schedule, refund/chargeback handling, tax responsibility, content takedown, moderation rights, termination, survival rights for already paid members.
