## 2. Points legal language

> Star Points là điểm thưởng nội bộ của DSTS Club. Star Points không phải tiền, không phải tài sản đầu tư, không phải chứng khoán, không phải crypto, không phải ví điện tử và không có quyền quy đổi thành tiền mặt trong giai đoạn đầu. Star Points chỉ được sử dụng để đổi quyền lợi, ưu đãi, quà tặng, vé, trải nghiệm hoặc đặc quyền nội bộ do DSTS công bố theo từng thời kỳ.

## 3. Fan membership rules

Fans pay for access, not ownership. Paid content cannot be copied, recorded, leaked, resold, reuploaded. DSTS can suspend accounts for abuse/fraud. Membership benefits may vary by creator. Some experiences are limited and not guaranteed unless confirmed.

## 4. Creator rules

Creator must agree they own or have rights to uploaded content, grant DSTS license to host/display/preview/promote/clip/distribute according to agreement, are responsible for statements/claims/privacy/copyright/third-party rights, and DSTS can moderate/restrict/remove/pause content.

## 5. Referral policy

Referral rewards are points, not cash. Points may be pending. Refund/fraud may claw back points. Self-referral is prohibited. Users sharing referral links must disclose they may receive rewards/points.

## 6. Reward policy

Rewards may have limited quantity, require eligibility, expire, change if equivalent alternative is offered. Experience rewards require schedule confirmation.

## 7. Event and talk show terms

Event format can change online/offline if needed. Guest schedule can change. Replay availability depends on content rights. Recording by users is prohibited unless allowed. Questions may be moderated.

## 8. Direct Experience terms

Apply-only, approval required, creator confirmation required, time/location/format/boundaries clear, DSTS can require staff/moderator presence, no unsafe requests.

## 9. Sponsor terms

Sponsor is not investment. Sponsorship is brand collaboration or support. No profit share unless separate legal agreement. No control over creator/community unless agreed.

## 10. Ambassador / Chapter Leader terms

Ambassador is community role, not employee unless contract states. No authority to collect money unless authorized. Must follow brand and conduct rules.

## 11. Mobile app terms

App access depends on account status. App purchase rules may vary by platform. Some purchases may be web-only depending on policy. Data collection disclosed. Account deletion request supported.

## 12. Hard-stop language

Do not say: điểm có thể đổi tiền, mua điểm là đầu tư, chắc chắn gặp riêng, creator chắc chắn kiếm tiền, fan chắc chắn được trả lời, không bao giờ mất điểm, tài trợ sẽ sinh lời, gói này giúp nổi tiếng.
